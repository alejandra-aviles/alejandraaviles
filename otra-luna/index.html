Otra luna, (other moon) 2014

Installation to attract nocturnal insects
Wire, electricity, light bulbs
Structure diameter: 200 cm
Action duration: 1 hour

Some insects use the moon for their orientation and displacement, they say that might be the reason why they get attracted to artificial light (positive phototaxis). I build an structure of lightbulbs, to attract the insects of the field during the night and confuse them about the real light of the moon.

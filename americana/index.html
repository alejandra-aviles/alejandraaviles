Alejandra Avilés Work Text CV

Americana, 2016

Drawing
Paper and ink
Dimensions: 100 x 70 cm and 70 x 50 cm

“Americana” is the feminine conjugation in spanish of “american”. Americana personifies the feminine dimension of a word that represents a continent. The initial reticulated intention is broken by the organic movement of the hand. I wanted to write a word charged with meaning and through the repetition dilute the meaning of the term.

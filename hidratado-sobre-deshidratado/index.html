Hydrated over dehydrated, 2016

Sculpture on the variations of hydration
Aubergines
Variable dimensions

The aubergine on top seems to be making weight on the bottom one, but there’s actually no weight, this is an impression given by the variations on the hydration and dehydration of the vegetable. I wanted to point out a hierarchical relation using the nourishment and exhaustion of the vegetable.

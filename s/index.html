Alejandra Avilés Work Text CV

S, 2016

Intervention on the edge of a wall
Silver Necklace
Dimensions: 50 cm (straight)

I put a silver necklace in the edge of an exhibition wall. It was a single day exhibition but my intervention didn’t last that long, as fastest I got distracted, the silver curve was gone. I’m not sure if necklace was stolen by someone in the public or if the snake slip away.

⌇

Kunsverein Rosa Luxemburg Platz
Saas-Fee Summer Institute of Art
July 2016
Berlin, Germany

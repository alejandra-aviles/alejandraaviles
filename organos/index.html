Organos, (organs) 2015

Photography (unknown author) on wood
Dimensions: 22 x 28 x 3cm

This is a photography that I find in the encyclopedia “the world that we live”. We see a pair of sahuaros, an specimen of cactus that slowly only grows in the Sonoran desert. I like nativeness and biodiversity aspects related to the image. I don’t have a track of the author, but I kept the image with me.

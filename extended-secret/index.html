Alejandra Avilés Work Text CV

Extended secret, 2012

Installation
Ioned plastic
Dimensions: 1100 x 700

I was invited to make an intervention on the central yard of the Sonora College on the ocation of the day against women's violence. This is the largest installation I’ve made, and I decided to make a membrane using the pink as reference to the interior of the skin.

The installation was suspended in the central yard, and the transparency is given by the exterior light entering into the yard. I only use plastic as a material. For the texture I made folds and ion them, I used the heat needed to melt the plastic and paste it without burning it, until complete the hole layer.

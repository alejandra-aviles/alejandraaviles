Alejandra Avilés Work Text CV

Impulso hacia arriba

Pine wood and cotton canvas
Dimensions: 3.34 x 1.90 x .73
Unpublished, Sonora, Mexico, 2018

The cot is the most used folding bed in Mexican rural areas. A nomadic element that mounts at night to sleep and dismounts in the morning before starting the day. The cot makes the transit between inside and outside, where you sleep mostly in the summer.

I made the extension of the legs of a cot to turn them limbs and give the sensation of elevation within the body of the cot. The sculpture is between the familiar and the strange.
